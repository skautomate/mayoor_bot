How to Use the Bot

Follow these simple steps to book a venue with our AI assistant:

  1. To begin, you can say "Hello" or select a quick option like "I want to book MP-Hall".

  2. The bot will ask for your booking details, including your name, class, and the venue.

  3. Next, provide the specific date and time for your event. The bot will automatically check for availability.

  4. You can also include any special requirements, such as a projector or microphones.

  5. Once all details are confirmed, the bot will generate a unique 4-digit booking code for you.

This code is essential for any future changes or cancellations to your booking.
